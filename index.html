The Misconception!

With the popularity of doomsday movies, it has led many to believe that meteorite landings on Earth are rare occasions. Many people also believe that when a meteorite does land on Earth, it is going to be a huge Earth destroying meteorite.

But is that really the truth about meteorite landings?
